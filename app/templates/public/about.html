Our Story

1. Who We Are

GLH — Greenfield Local Hub — is a Milton Keynes based cooperative platform connecting local farmers and independent food producers directly with customers in their community. We are not just a delivery service. We are a movement built around the belief that food should be local, honest, and fairly traded.

Founded in 2024, GLH was born out of a simple frustration — local farmers and producers had incredible products but no central, modern platform to sell them through. Meanwhile, customers wanted fresher, more ethical alternatives to supermarkets but had nowhere to find them conveniently. GLH bridges that gap.

Purpose

2. Our Mission

Our mission is to make local food accessible to everyone while ensuring the people who grow and produce it are fairly compensated. We believe that shortening the supply chain is better for farmers, better for customers, and better for the planet.

Fair Trade Vendors keep the majority of every sale
Local First Every product on GLH comes from within our region
Community Built by and for the Milton Keynes community
The Process

3. How It Works

GLH operates as both a physical hub and a digital marketplace. At our Milton Keynes location, vendors bring their produce and products to a central space where customers can shop in person. Our online platform extends this — letting customers browse, order, and have products delivered from the same trusted vendors.

01
Vendors apply

Local farmers and producers apply through our contact page. Our admin team reviews and approves applications.

02
Products go live

Approved vendors list their products on the GLH platform with prices, descriptions, and stock levels.

03
Customers order

Customers browse, favourite, and order products for delivery or collection from our hub.

04
Vendors fulfil

Orders go directly to the vendor who fulfils and dispatches. GLH handles payments and customer support.

Our People

4. Our Vendors

Every vendor on GLH is independently verified by our admin team. We work exclusively with local farmers, smallholders, artisan food producers, and independent drink makers based in and around Milton Keynes and the surrounding counties.

Vendors on our platform range from small family-run farms supplying seasonal vegetables, to independent meat suppliers, dairy producers, bakers, and drinks makers. Each vendor has their own storefront on GLH so customers always know exactly who they are buying from and where their food comes from.

Interested in selling on GLH? Visit our contact page to apply.

Roots

5. Our Community

GLH is deeply rooted in the Milton Keynes community. We partner with local schools, colleges, and community organisations to run food education events, farm visits, and seasonal markets. We believe that reconnecting people with where their food comes from creates healthier communities and more sustainable food systems.

A portion of every transaction on GLH goes into a community fund used to support local food banks, school nutrition programmes, and agricultural training for young people in our region.

Environment

6. Sustainability

Sustainability is not a marketing term for us — it is a core operating principle. By connecting customers directly to local producers, we eliminate unnecessary supply chain steps that generate carbon emissions. All deliveries made through GLH are optimised by zone to minimise vehicle journeys.

We actively encourage vendors to use recyclable and compostable packaging and provide guidance and resources to help them do so. Our physical hub is operated using renewable energy and we are working towards becoming a fully carbon-neutral operation by 2027.
