Pricing

1. Delivery Charges

We want getting your favourite local produce to your door to be as affordable as possible. Our delivery charges are as follows:

Standard Delivery £3.95 2–3 working days
Next Day Delivery £6.95 Order before 12pm
Click & Collect Free From our MK hub

Free standard delivery is available on all orders over £35.

Timing

2. Delivery Times

For most of the UK, standard delivery takes 2–3 working days from the day your order is placed. For example, an order placed on Monday will typically arrive by Wednesday or Thursday.

Next day delivery is available for orders placed before 12pm Monday to Friday. Orders placed after 12pm will be dispatched the following working day.

Delivery Type Estimated Time Cut-off Time
Standard 2–3 working days Anytime
Next Day Next working day 12:00pm
Click & Collect Same day available 2 hours notice
Restrictions

3. Delivery Restrictions

Currently GLH delivers to addresses within the United Kingdom only. We are unable to deliver to PO Boxes or BFPO addresses at this time.

Some remote postcodes in Scotland, the Scottish Highlands, Northern Ireland, and certain island postcodes may experience longer delivery times of up to 5 working days. You will be notified at checkout if your postcode falls into this category.

Certain fresh and chilled products may have additional delivery restrictions based on temperature requirements. These will be clearly marked on the product page.

Sustainability

4. Packaging

At GLH we are committed to sustainable packaging. All our orders are packed securely in recyclable cardboard boxes. Chilled and fresh produce is packed using biodegradable insulated liners and ice packs where necessary to maintain temperature during transit.

We actively encourage our vendors to use minimal, recyclable, or compostable packaging for their products. Where possible, we consolidate multiple items from the same vendor into a single package to reduce waste.

We are working towards fully plastic-free packaging across all GLH orders by 2026.

Tracking

5. Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive an email confirmation containing your tracking information. You can use this to follow your order in real time through our delivery partner's tracking portal.

You will also receive a notification when your order is out for delivery and again when it has been delivered. If you have a GLH account, you can view your order status at any time from your dashboard.

If you have not received tracking information within 24 hours of placing your order, please contact us at hello@glh.co.uk.

Planning

6. Seasonal Changes

During peak periods such as Christmas, Easter, and Bank Holidays, delivery times may be extended. We will always communicate any expected delays clearly on our website and via email before you place your order.

Some seasonal products may have limited availability or adjusted delivery windows. We recommend placing orders for seasonal items well in advance during busy periods to avoid disappointment.

Collection

7. Click & Collect

Prefer to collect your order in person? Our Click & Collect service lets you order online and pick up from our Milton Keynes hub at no extra cost.

Collection Point Chaffron Way, Milton Keynes, MK6 5LP
Collection Hours Mon–Fri: 9am–10pm · Sat–Sun: 10am–11pm
Notice Required Please allow at least 2 hours after ordering

You will receive an email when your order is ready for collection. Please bring your order confirmation email or GLH account reference when collecting.

Issues

8. Failed Deliveries

If a delivery attempt is unsuccessful, our delivery partner will leave a card and attempt redelivery the following working day. After two failed attempts, your order will be held at a local collection point for up to 7 days.

If we are unable to deliver your order and it is returned to us, we will contact you to arrange redelivery. A redelivery charge may apply. Perishable items that cannot be redelivered will be subject to our refund policy.

If any items in your order arrive damaged or are missing, please contact us within 48 hours at hello@glh.co.uk and we will resolve this as quickly as possible.
