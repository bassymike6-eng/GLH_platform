Delivery

Delivery

GLH currently delivers to all addresses within the United Kingdom. We do not currently deliver internationally. Some remote postcodes in Scotland, the Highlands, and certain islands may experience longer delivery times.

Standard delivery costs £3.95. Next day delivery is available for £6.95 on orders placed before 12pm. Orders over £35 qualify for free standard delivery. Click & Collect from our Milton Keynes hub is always free.

Yes — our Click & Collect service is free. You can collect your order from our hub at Chaffron Way, Milton Keynes, MK6 5LP. Please allow at least 2 hours after placing your order before collecting. You will receive a confirmation email when your order is ready.

Standard delivery takes 2–3 working days. Next day delivery arrives the following working day if ordered before 12pm. You will receive a tracking email once your order has been dispatched so you can follow it in real time.

If your delivery attempt is unsuccessful, our courier will leave a card and attempt redelivery the next working day. After two failed attempts your parcel will be held at a local collection point for up to 7 days. Contact us at hello@glh.co.uk if you have any issues.

Orders

Orders

You can cancel or amend your order within 1 hour of placing it by contacting us at hello@glh.co.uk. After this time your order may already be in preparation with the vendor and we cannot guarantee changes can be made.

Products

Products

Vendors

Vendors

You'll need a GLH customer account first. Once you have one, visit our Contact page and submit a vendor application. Our admin team will review your application and respond within 5 working days.

GLH operates on a small commission model — we take a percentage of each sale to cover platform costs. The exact rate will be communicated to you during the vendor application process. There are no upfront or monthly listing fees.

You will receive a confirmation email and your account will be upgraded to a vendor account. You can then log in to your vendor dashboard where you can create your shop, add products, manage stock, and view orders.

Account

Account

Click Sign Up from any page on the GLH platform. You'll need to provide your name, date of birth, and email address, then verify your email with a one-time code. The whole process takes under 2 minutes.

Click Forgot Password on the login page and enter your email address. We'll send you a verification code. Once verified, you can set a new password. If you don't receive the code within a few minutes, check your spam folder.

You can request account deletion by contacting us at hello@glh.co.uk. We will process your request within 30 days in line with our privacy policy. Please note that any active orders must be completed before your account can be deleted.

Returns & Refunds

Returns & Refunds

Due to the perishable nature of most products sold on GLH, we are unable to accept returns of food items unless they arrive damaged or incorrect. Non-perishable items may be returned within 14 days in original condition. Contact us at hello@glh.co.uk to arrange a return.

If your order arrives damaged, incorrect, or incomplete, contact us within 48 hours with your order reference and photos where possible. We will issue a refund or replacement within 5 working days. Refunds are returned to your original payment method.

If your tracking shows delivered but you haven't received your order, or if it has been more than 5 working days since dispatch with no update, please contact us immediately at hello@glh.co.uk. We will investigate and issue a replacement or full refund.
