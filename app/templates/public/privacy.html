1. Contact Details

GLH Food & Drink is committed to protecting your privacy. If you have any queries about this policy or how we handle your information, please contact us at hello@glh.co.uk or write to us at GLH Food & Drink, Chaffron Way, Milton Keynes, MK6 5LP.

2. Payments

All payment transactions on GLH are encrypted and processed securely. We do not store your full card details on our servers. Payment data is handled by trusted third-party payment processors who comply with PCI DSS standards. We retain transaction records only as required by law.

3. Cookies

When you visit our platform, we may store cookies in your browser to improve your experience. These include session cookies which expire when you close your browser, and preference cookies which remember your settings. You can control cookie behaviour through your browser settings. Rejecting cookies may limit some features of GLH.

4. Mobile Communication

We may use your contact details to send you order updates, delivery notifications, and service messages. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by updating your account preferences or clicking unsubscribe in any email we send. Essential service messages cannot be opted out of while you have an active account.

5. Information We Collect

When you use GLH, we collect information to provide and improve our services. This includes:

  • Vendors you've viewed and products you've browsed, to personalise recommendations
  • Location and IP address, to estimate delivery availability
  • Shipping address, to process and deliver your orders
  • Device and browser data, to ensure compatibility and security

We also use cookies to maintain your session while browsing so items remain in your basket.

6. Security

We adopt robust technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, accessed in an unauthorised way, altered, or disclosed. Access to your information is limited to staff and vendors who have a legitimate business need. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You can request an export of your data, ask us to restrict processing, or withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@glh.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days. This does not affect any data we are required to retain for legal or administrative reasons.

8. Changes to Our Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Where changes are significant, we will notify you by email or via a prominent notice on our platform. We encourage you to review this page periodically. This policy was last updated April 2026.
