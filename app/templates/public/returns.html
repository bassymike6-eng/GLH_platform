Returns & Refund Policy

At GLH we want every order to reach you in perfect condition. We understand that sometimes things go wrong and we are committed to resolving any issues as quickly and fairly as possible. Please read this policy carefully so you know your rights.

1. Overview

Because GLH is a marketplace for fresh, local, and perishable food products, our returns policy differs from standard retail. The nature of food means we cannot accept returns in the way a clothing or electronics retailer might. However we take quality and customer satisfaction seriously and will always work to make things right.

If you have an issue with your order, please contact us within 48 hours of receiving it at hello@glh.co.uk. Claims made after this window may not be eligible for a refund.

2. Perishable Items

Due to the nature of fresh and perishable food products — including fruits, vegetables, meats, fish, dairy, and bakery items — we are unable to accept physical returns of these goods once delivered.

However if a perishable item arrives in an unsatisfactory condition, damaged, spoiled, or significantly different from what was described, we will offer a full refund or replacement at no additional cost. You do not need to return the item.

To process a claim on a perishable item please contact us within 48 hours with:

  • Your order reference number
  • A description of the issue
  • Photos of the affected item where possible

3. Non-Perishable Items

Non-perishable products such as dried goods, spices, nuts, drinks in sealed packaging, and similar items may be returned within 14 days of delivery provided they are:

  • Unused and in their original condition
  • In original undamaged packaging
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase

To arrange a return of a non-perishable item please contact us at hello@glh.co.uk. We will provide you with a returns address and instructions. Return postage costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is faulty or was sent in error.

4. Damaged Orders

If your order arrives damaged — whether the packaging is crushed, items have leaked, or products have been broken in transit — please do not dispose of the items before contacting us.

We ask that you contact us within 48 hours of delivery with your order reference and photos of the damage. Based on the nature of the damage we will offer either a full refund, a partial refund, or a replacement order dispatched as soon as possible.

Damage claims are taken seriously and we report repeated transit damage issues to our delivery partners to prevent recurrence.

5. Missing Items

If items are missing from your order please check your dispatch confirmation email first — in some cases items from different vendors may be sent in separate packages and arrive at different times.

If an item is confirmed missing and not arriving in a separate shipment, please contact us within 48 hours. We will investigate with the relevant vendor and either dispatch the missing item or issue a refund for it.

GLH keeps detailed dispatch records and will work quickly to resolve any missing item claims.

6. Refunds

Once a refund has been approved it will be processed within 5 working days. Refunds are returned to the original payment method used at checkout. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or card provider.

Situation Outcome Timeframe
Damaged perishable item Full refund or replacement 5 working days
Missing item Replacement or refund 5 working days
Non-perishable return Refund on receipt 5 working days
Order not delivered Full refund 5 working days
Cancelled order Full refund 3 working days

If you have not received your refund within the timeframe stated above please contact us and we will investigate immediately.

7. Contact Us

For all returns, refund requests, or order issues please get in touch with our team. We aim to respond to all queries within 24 hours Monday to Friday.
